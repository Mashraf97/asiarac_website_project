Meet AsiaRAC

Asia Retail Academy (AsiaRAC) is a group of international business leaders, trainers, and coaches from various fields. With over 100 years of combined experience, we have worked with organizations and leaders in Malaysia, Singapore, Oman, Myanmar, India, Thailand, Indonesia, China, and the UK. We continue to expand our services across Asia. Our goal is to empower people in Asia through ongoing training and upskilling that is relevant, applicable, and current.
